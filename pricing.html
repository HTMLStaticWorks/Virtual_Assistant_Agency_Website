Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our billing and service terms.

Is there a setup fee?

No, we do not charge any setup or recruitment fees. You only pay for the monthly service hours.

How do I track hours?

All virtual assistants use our proprietary dashboard for time tracking, which you can review in real-time.

What is the notice period?

We work on a month-to-month basis. You can cancel or change your plan with a 30-day notice.

Can I interview the VA?

Yes! For our Half-Time and Full-Time plans, we provide candidate profiles and facilitate intro calls.
