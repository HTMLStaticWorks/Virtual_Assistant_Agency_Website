Frequently Asked Questions

What services do your virtual assistants provide?

Our virtual assistants handle admin support, inbox management, calendar scheduling, data entry, customer service, bookkeeping, and basic digital marketing tasks.

How does the billing and hour tracking work?

Hours are tracked through our proprietary dashboard. You are billed monthly in advance for your selected plan hours, which reset at the start of each billing cycle.

Can I cancel or change my plan anytime?

Yes, we work on a month-to-month basis. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription with a simple 30-day notice through your account manager.

How are candidates vetted and trained?

We hire only the top 2% of applicants through a rigorous 4-stage screening process. All VAs receive specialized training in security and premium business tools before being matched.
