About Virtual Assistant Agency

We believe in empowering entrepreneurs and growing businesses by connecting them with world-class remote talent.

Our Mission

Founded in 2024, Virtual Assistant Agency was created to solve a problem we faced ourselves: drowning in administrative work instead of focusing on what actually moves the needle.

We built a platform that not only matches you with top 1% virtual assistant talent but provides the infrastructure (dashboards, time tracking, secure messaging) to make managing a remote team effortless.

Our Core Values

Transparency

No hidden fees, no complex contracts. You see exactly what you pay for and the exact hours worked.

Excellence

We vet thousands of applicants to accept only the top 1%. Your work deserves the best.

Security

Your data is protected. Our platform uses enterprise-grade security for file sharing and communications.
